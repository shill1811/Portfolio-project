About Me

My journey into the tech world commenced a year ago when I decided to lean HTML, CSS and JavaScrip on my own. Over the last three months, I've been actively honing my skills with the invaluable guidance and support from a group called ('Because She Can' Mentorship Program) and my dedicated mentor assinged to me by the BSC program. I've acquired a solid foundation and am enthusiastic about my ongoing learning and growth in the months and years to come and I'm excited to share my first self-made project, which is my portfolio website.

While you explore my profile, I warmly welcome any opportunities, whether they involve internships or further training. I'm open to a wide range of possibilities and am excited to leave my mark on the tech industry. Keep an eye on my journey!
